About Our Madrasah

Tamirul Millat Kamil Madrasa is a religious higher education institution in Bangladesh. Managed by the Tamirul Millat Trust, this madrasa has not only secured top positions among all the madrasas in the country but has also consistently shown excellent results in the entrance exams of major universities across the country, including the University of Dhaka.
